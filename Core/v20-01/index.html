PREFIXES

.m3-[utility].m3-[component]

Who would like a framework that messes up with your project's pre-existing styles?

That is why we use prefixes instead!

Prefixes allow you to use everything M3 has to offer without having to worry about overriding your custom classes :)

INTUITIVE

m3-[component] m3-[background] m3-text-[color]

Why creating multiple classes for similar purposes?

Whether you want a blue button or a red navbar, there's no need to struggle with classes like .btn-primary or .navbar-dark

Move on from those unnecessary classes that you will never use. It's time for some refreshing coding!
