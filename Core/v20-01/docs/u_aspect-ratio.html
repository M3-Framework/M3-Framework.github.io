Aspect Ratio

Crops the image to preserve the ratio of a container.

Ratio: 16:9

Table of Available Ratios
Class Description
.m3-aspect-ratio .is-1-1 Ratio 1:1
.m3-aspect-ratio .is-3-2 Ratio 3:2
.m3-aspect-ratio .is-4-3 Ratio 4:3
.m3-aspect-ratio .is-8-5 Ratio 8:5
.m3-aspect-ratio .is-16-9 Ratio 16:9
Usage:

<figure class="m3-aspect-ratio is-1-1">
    <img src="img_src" alt="...">
    <figcaption class="m3-display-bottom m3-tag">Tag</figcaption>
</figure>
Reminder: The colors used in this documentation are part of M3 Colors.
If you don't require our library, remember to replace each color with your own.
