Pure CSS

Photo Film

maximum width and height: 250px

Display series of images or videos inside a responsive container.

Example:

<section class="m3-photofilm m3-black">
    <figure class="m3-figure">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/400/400" alt="Example Image"/>
        <figcaption class="m3-caption">Title</figcaption>
    </figure>

    <figure class="m3-figure">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/500/400" alt="Example Image"/>
        <figcaption class="m3-caption">Title</figcaption>
    </figure>
    
    <figure class="m3-figure">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/600/600" alt="Example Image"/>
        <figcaption class="m3-caption">Title</figcaption>
    </figure>

    <figure class="m3-figure">
        <img src="https://picsum.photos/400/300" alt="Example Image"/>
        <figcaption class="m3-caption">Title</figcaption>
    </figure>
</section>
Reminder: The colors used in this documentation are part of M3 Colors.
If you don't require our library, remember to replace each color with your own.
