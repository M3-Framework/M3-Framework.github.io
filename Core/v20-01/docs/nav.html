Toolbar

z-index: 2

Provides an easy access to quick-actions or tools for your site.


<div class="m3-toolbar">
    <input type="checkbox" id="AnyID"/>
    <label for="AnyID" class="m3-toggle"></label>
    <ul class="m3-tools">
        <li class="m3-option m3-light-gray m3-circle"><a class="fas fa-mug-hot"></a></li>
        <li class="m3-option m3-light-gray m3-circle"><a class="fas fa-cog"></a></li>
        <li class="m3-option m3-light-gray m3-circle"><a class="far fa-thumbs-up"></a></li>
        <li class="m3-option m3-light-gray m3-circle"><a class="far fa-trash-alt"></a></li>
    </ul>
</div>

You may add the class .reverse to the .m3-toolbar to place it on the right corner:

<div class="m3-toolbar reverse">...</div>
Reminder: The colors used in this documentation are part of M3 Colors.
If you don't require our library, remember to replace each color with your own.
